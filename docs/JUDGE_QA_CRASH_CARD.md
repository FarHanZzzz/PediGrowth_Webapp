# Pedi-Growth Judge Q&A Crash Card

Use these exact responses under pressure. Keep answers short, honest, and consistent.

## Core Positioning

One-liner:
GAITBRIDGE helps caregivers capture a usable walking video, explains what was observed in simple language, and creates a clinician-ready handoff packet for follow-up review.

Boundary line:
This is a support workflow for documentation and follow-up preparation, not a diagnostic system.

## Top 10 Judge Questions and Fast Answers

1. Is this diagnosing CP?
Answer: No. We do not diagnose conditions. We provide structured observational summaries and clinician handoff support.

2. What happens when video quality is poor?
Answer: Quality gates downgrade confidence or reject analysis. We show retake guidance instead of forcing unreliable output.

3. Why should clinicians trust this?
Answer: Every output includes evidence, limits, and clear follow-up framing. We expose uncertainty instead of hiding it.

4. What is your real product wedge?
Answer: Capture to explain to clinician handoff in one workflow that families can use from a phone.

5. Why not build full diagnosis AI now?
Answer: Safety and credibility. We prioritize reliable workflow value now and expand with stronger validation later.

6. What prevents overclaiming?
Answer: Policy-guarded language, non-diagnostic framing, and visible limitations across parent and clinician outputs.

7. What does the clinician packet add?
Answer: Quick scan of concern profile, confidence context, limitations, and next-step actions in a structured handoff format.

8. Can this work across devices?
Answer: Yes. We create secure, time-limited share links and provide printable/exportable packet fallbacks.

9. What is your next milestone after hackathon?
Answer: Deploy hardening, workflow validation with real users, and stronger longitudinal review support.

10. Why does this win now?
Answer: It solves a real handoff gap today with an honest, usable workflow instead of promising magic AI.

## Red-Line Phrases (Never Say)

- "This diagnoses CP"
- "Clinically proven accuracy" (unless validated and documented)
- "Medical-grade certainty"
- "Guaranteed detection"

## Closing Statement

We are building the safest useful version first: one clip, one calm summary, one structured handoff, and one clear next step.
